A modular app platform aimed at small businesses that wanted a branded mobile and web presence without commissioning custom development. Owners signed up, picked a template, and filled out a configuration form to hydrate it with their content and brand. Composable integration bricks layered real functionality on top — a Toast POS integration for menu and ordering, a built-in rewards/punchcard system for customer loyalty, and a creator portal where technical users could craft new data-hydrated templates with a drag-and-drop interface for the rest of the platform to reuse.
